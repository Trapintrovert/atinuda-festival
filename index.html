About Atinuda

The Atinuda Festival is proposed to be an annual gathering of artists, artisans and creatives across Ogun State. Our core objective is to create a networking platform for young creatives to gain access to mentors, funding opportunities, employment, art grants/ scholarships, investors and project commissions. The festival will foster local and international Cultural exchange and spur a new energy across the Art & Culture ecosystem annually in Ogun State.
